	<h3><strong>Enhancements</strong></h3>
	<ul>
		<li><strong>CI: Vendors &amp; Manufacturers Added to Product Views</strong> - Designers now can access the name of a product manufacturer/vendor directly in the viewset without adding any new code. (<a href="https://github.com/HotcakesCommerce/hotcakes-commerce-core/issues/311" target="_blank">Issue 311</a>)</li>
		<li><strong>Support for Submit Buttons in Viewsets</strong> - Designers now can use and target submit buttons in their viewset. (<a href="https://github.com/HotcakesCommerce/hotcakes-commerce-core/pull/309" target="_blank">Issue 309</a>)</li>
		<li><strong>Enhanced Support for EU B2B Stores</strong> - EU-based stores can now enjoy proper formatting &amp; validation of their respective VAT number. (<a href="https://github.com/HotcakesCommerce/hotcakes-commerce-core/issues/155" target="_blank">Issue 155</a>)</li>
		<li><strong>Initial Support for Prompt Commands</strong> - You can now view products, categories, and a sales summary using Prompt in the persona bar. (No Issue)</li>
	</ul>
